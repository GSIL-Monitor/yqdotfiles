Activating or deactivating all rules at once

You can activate or deactive a rule by clicking its checkbox. Here's a shortcut to do all the rules for the current folder at once:

To activate or deactivate all rules at once:

  1. Hold down the command key while clicking on a checkbox.
  2. If the rule being clicked on was active before, all the rules are deactivated.
  3. If the rule being clicked on was inactive before, all the rules are activated.
