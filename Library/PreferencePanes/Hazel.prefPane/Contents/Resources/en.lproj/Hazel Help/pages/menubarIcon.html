Hazel's menu bar icon

Hazel's menu bar icon gives you immediate indication of its status as well as quick access to some of its functions, such as starting/stopping and running rules manually.

The icon has two states to indicate Hazel's status:

Hazel is running

Hazel is stopped

Clicking on the icon will reveal a menu where you can quickly access other functions

To enable the menu bar icon:

  1. Navigate to the "Info" pane.
  2. Check "Show Hazel status in the menu bar".
