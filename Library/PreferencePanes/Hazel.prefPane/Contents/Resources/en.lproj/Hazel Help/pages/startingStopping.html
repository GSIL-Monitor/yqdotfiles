Stopping and restarting Hazel

Normally, Hazel is always watching your folders and running your rules as needed. Sometimes, though, you may need to stop Hazel temporarily. For instance, if you want to test out some experimental rules and want to preview them first.

To stop Hazel:

  1. Navigate to the "Info" pane.
  2. Click on the "Stop Hazel" button.
  3. The status should change to reflect Hazel's stopped state.

Alternatively, if you have Hazel showing its status in the menu bar:

  1. Click on Hazel's menu bar icon.
  2. Select the "Stop Hazel" item.
  3. The icon should change to reflect the stopped state.

To start Hazel again:

  1. Click the button or menu bar item (which should now read "Start Hazel") again.

While Hazel is stopped, no rules or Trash processing will occur.
