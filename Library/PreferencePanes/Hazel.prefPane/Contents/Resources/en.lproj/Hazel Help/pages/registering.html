Registering Hazel

If you have received a license (full or evaluation), you can unlock Hazel's full feature set. An evaluation license will unlock Hazel for a limited amount of time whereas a full license has no expiration. A full license can be purchased by clicking on the "Purchase" button in the "Info" pane.

To register Hazel:

  1. Double-click the license file.

or

  1. Drag the license file into Hazel's window.

or

  1. Go to the "Info" pane.
  2. Click the plus (+) button next to the license field and find the license file on your drive.

Upon successful registration, you should see the name under which you registered. If you have an evaluation license, the expiration date will be shown as well.
